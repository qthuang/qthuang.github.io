Facial Expression Classification

Introduction

This is the final project of CS523 Deep Learning class. I did some experiments and modifications on the framework presented by the paper "Extended deep neural network for facial emotion recognition" in Pattern Recognition Letters. First, I reproduced the framework presented in the paper. Then, by changing the arrangement of convolution layers, pooling layers and residual blocks, I experimented with the new framework and evaluated them concerning accuracy and time consumption.
