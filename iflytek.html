IFLYGO - Educational Go Bot

Introduction

IFLYGO (讯飞玄机) is an intelligent robot for Go teaching, training and entertainment. It integrates a number of technologies in the fields of perceptual intelligence, cognitive intelligence and motor intelligence, and possesses rich knowledge of Go rules and etiquette, with a level of play that surpasses that of top human players.
